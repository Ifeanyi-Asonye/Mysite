# About Me

Hey there! I'm Ifeanyi, and I've got a pretty interesting journey that's taken me from retail shelves to server rooms.

I spent 6 years in retail, working my way up from shelf attendant to store supervisor. But here's the thing—I was always the person my colleagues turned to when something went wrong with the computers. So after realizing that's where my real passion was, I went back to school and got my degree in Computer Science.

That led me to an educational organization where I started as an IT support intern, then moved into the helpdesk. Within a few months, I landed a full-time role as a system administrator—and I've been thriving there for the past 3 years.

So whether you're wondering why I switched careers or just want to chat about tech, IT infrastructure, or even retail stories, I'm here for it. All told, I've got 10 years of work experience under my belt, and I'm still learning something new every day.
