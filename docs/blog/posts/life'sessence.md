# Life's Essence

They say life is a fleeting breath, a momentary dance beneath the sun. 

We are constantly chasing dreams, sprinting through the days, climbing mountains, 

and crafting legacies, one after another.

<!-- more -->

But in this race for more, for better, for higher, we forget the silent whispers of the wind, 

the soft hum of the earth beneath our feet, and the beauty in the pause where it all started.

Life is not just the goal or the end, not in the trophies or the endless chase. 

It's in the rhythm of the everyday breaths, the smile that lights up a stranger's face. 

It's in the quiet mornings or the lazy nights, when the world is still young and tender, 

in the way the stars dance at night, reminding us that we all share the same space.

But we run, we rush, we race through the hours, 

never stopping to taste the sweetness of now, ignoring the bloom of today for tomorrow's towers, 

forgetting to live, forgetting to laugh.

Yet, in the end, when the race has been run, when our stories are sealed, and our songs are all sung, we realize it wasn't the heights or the speed, but the moments we missed, where life truly starts.

Dear You, 

On this journey called life, do stop sometimes to smell the flowers while you can, 

because you won't be able to, even when your graveside is surrounded by them.

## Live, laugh, and love life.
